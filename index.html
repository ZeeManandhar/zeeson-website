About Us

TimberArt Nepal is a traditional store chain based in Kathmandu, specializing in handmade handicrafts crafted by skilled artisans. Nestled in the vibrant streets of Thamel, we offer a unique collection of wooden carvings, metal statues, and local fabrics that reflect Nepal's rich culture and heritage.

Our mission is to preserve the beauty of Nepalese craftsmanship while connecting it to the world. Whether you're a local enthusiast or an international tourist, TimberArt Nepal invites you to explore and take home a piece of Nepal.
